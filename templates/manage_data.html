Excel File Format Guide
Instructions: Place your Excel files (.xlsx or .xls) in the data directory. The system will automatically load the most recent file.
Required Excel Columns:
Patient Information
  • • PatientID or Patient ID
  • • Name
  • • Gender
  • • Date of Birth or Age
  • • MRN
  • • Address
  • • Contact Number or Phone
Medical Information
  • • Primary ICU Diagnosis
  • • Secondary Diagnoses
  • • Allergies
  • • ICU Admission Date
  • • ICU Discharge Date
  • • Length of Stay (Days)
Medication & Care
  • • Medication
  • • Dosage
  • • Frequency
  • • Route
  • • Prescriber Name
  • • NPI Number
  • • Skilled Nursing Needed
  • • Nursing Visit Frequency
Equipment & Services
  • • Equipment Needed
  • • DME Supplier
  • • Physical Therapy
  • • Transportation Needed
  • • Insurance Coverage Status
  • • Special Instructions
Flexible Column Names: The system supports multiple column name variations (e.g., "PatientID", "Patient ID", "patient_id" all work for patient identification).
